Our History

Founded in 1986 by industry experts Paul R. Gannon and William P. Gannon III Esq. Gannon Building & Remodeling is equipped in construction management and total development services for a wide range of commercial & residential projects. Our commercial project list is composed of Office Buildings, Big-Box Retail, Specialty Retail and Historical Renovations.

Gannon Building & Remodeling offers award winning quality,cost effective construction, efficient project management and strong construction knowledge to provide everything essential for all types of commercial & residential building.

With a strong team of strategic players that include: Project Managers, Estimators, Field Supervisors , and a vast base of subcontractors, suppliers and financial institutuions, we have the capability to handle multi-million-dollar projects down to the smallest of renovations.No job is too big or small; they have an equal importance at any contract value. Our team offers our clients a local presence and all the necessary components it takes to hold a successful track record.

Testimonial

    Gannon Building Building provides expertise to provide every client with the following services:


  • Review preliminary and final construction drawings with architect/engineer to provide value engineering/ cost savings suggestions.

  • Prepare a construction budget based on architectural drawings to be utilized as a basis for sub-trade buy-outs and purchasing.

  • Prepare a bidding or all sub-trades that is specific in scope and provides assurance of accurate sub-trade bids.

  • Make cost savings suggestions relating to lowest big vs. scheduling

  • Interface with all sub-trades, suppliers, and architect/ engineer,freeing owner from day-to-day supervision.

  • Execute all sub-contracts and provide submittals for review and approval.
